Event description

Description

Women Who Code (WWCode) is changing the face of tech, creating a more inclusive and diverse industry, and helping women to connect with professional opportunities that propel them into positions of power.

Our premier technical and developer conference, WWCode CONNECT 2017 does just that.


Conference attendees will experience the best technical and career development workshops Women Who Code has to offer, as well as lightning talks from the tech industry’s most influential leaders and more. Join us for in-depth panels, technical workshops, keynotes, and actionable advice on advancing more women into technology careers.

The conference will also feature unique opportunities, such as networking events and the WWCode career fair, where you can meet companies interested in hiring and developing tech women.

This event will sell out. Purchase tickets now. #WeRise ​
