What is a CSC Code?

CSC Code is a security code used to protect your card and prevent fraudulent use.

Visa/MasterCard/Discover

On Visa, MasterCard, Discover the CSC Code is the last 3 digits immediately after your card number on the back of your card. The CSC Code is located within the signature panel (see examples below).

American Express

On American Express cards the CSC Code is the 4 digits above and to the right of your card number on the front of your card (see example below).
